

## About Arinda Iradi

Arinda Iradi is a computer scientist and AI developer with a deep commitment to building intelligent systems that solve real-world challenges. His work sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence, data science, and web development, driven by a belief that technology should be accessible, impactful, and human-centered.

With a strong foundation in machine learning, Arinda develops applications that go beyond automation—they learn, adapt, and empower. His recent projects include an AI Agriculture Tutor that provides farmers with real-time crop advice and pest detection using natural language processing and computer vision, and a Business Performance Dashboard that helps small and medium enterprises visualize key metrics and make data-driven decisions. He has also built a blockchain-based voting system (TrustVote) to enhance transparency and trust in digital governance, alongside interactive educational tools like a 3D Compiler Visualizer that makes complex computer science concepts tangible for learners.

Arinda's approach is defined by curiosity and pragmatism. He combines technical depth with a user-first mindset, ensuring that every solution is not just functional, but genuinely useful. Whether he's deploying models on the edge, architecting cloud-native applications, or exploring the frontiers of NLP and computer vision, his goal remains consistent: to create technology that matters.

Beyond the code, Arinda is a passionate advocate for tech-for-good initiatives, believing that innovation thrives when it serves communities. He is constantly learning, collaborating, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible—always with an eye toward making a meaningful difference.
