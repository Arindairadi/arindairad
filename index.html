My Journey in Tech

A brief reflection on what drives my work.

Arinda Iradi is a computer scientist and AI developer passionate about building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. His work spans machine learning, data science, and web development, with a focus on creating practical, human-centered technology.

Recent projects include an AI Agriculture Tutor that provides farmers with real-time crop advice and pest detection, and a Business Performance Dashboard that helps SMEs visualize key metrics. He has also developed TrustVote, a blockchain-based voting system for transparent digital governance, and a 3D Compiler Visualizer that makes complex computer science concepts accessible to learners.

"Driven by curiosity and a commitment to tech-for-good, I combine technical depth with a user-first mindset. My goal is simple: build technology that matters, empowers communities, and pushes the boundaries of innovation."
